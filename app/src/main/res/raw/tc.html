Privacy Policy

 

 

The following Privacy Policy statement is intended to help you understand how Christian Life Coach uses the information that you decide to share, so that you can ultimately decided if you want to share it.  From this point on, Christian Life Coach, located at DrKeironBrown.com will be known, collectively, as “Christian Life Coach” or “CLC”.

 

At Christian Life Coach, our positions on privacy are pretty straightforward, and they exist to protect each and every user, so that they can all enjoy themselves, grow as people, and do so without having to worry about anyone hurting them or doing harm or discomfort to others.

 

  1. The information that you provide in any part of the CLC app or its components or content is yours; it belongs to you. As such, you need to be extremely careful as to what you put in them.  We know that it’s fun to journal and write things that you use and share with your friends, family, and friends-to-be, that’s one of the reasons we created Christian Life Coach.  However, there’s a big “BUT,” here: BUT we want you to be safe.  That means that you have to be very careful about putting any information into CLC that some people, maybe some unsafe people, might use against you, may use to hurt you, or may use to find you.  That’s nothing that anyone at Christian Life Coach wants, and nothing that you or your family want, either.  Please take appropriate care and caution regarding what you write, send, share, display and use as it relates to CLC.

 

  1. Besides your email address, there is no other personal or identifying information from you or about you is saved on our servers. Any other personal, identifying or financial data resides solely with you. We have no access to any of that data.  We NEVER sell or provide that information to any third party for the purpose of selling you anything, tracking you for commercial purposes, or for any kind of marketing reasons.

 

  1. The intended user of Christian Life Coach is at least 18 years old or the age of majority in their respective locales. We strongly recommend that any minors that access this site in any way ask their parents’ permission before joining us, or before sending any information about themselves to anyone over the Internet.
